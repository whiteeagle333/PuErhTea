We are a Family



Pu Erh Tea - Bringing High-Quality Pu Erh Tea to You from Our Cooperative of Organic Farmers. At Pu Erh, we are passionate about sharing the unique and complex flavors of Pu Erh tea with tea lovers around the world.
We work directly with a cooperative of organic farmers in the Yunnan province of China to source the highest quality tea leaves and bring them to you in their purest form. Our commitment to organic farming practices ensures that our teas are free from harmful chemicals and pesticides, and that they are grown in a way that is sustainable for the environment and the farmers who work the land.
By supporting our cooperative of working farmers, we are able to bring you the best possible Pu Erh tea while also supporting a community of farmers who are dedicated to producing high-quality, organic teas. Our selection of Pu'er tea includes both raw and ripe varieties, each with their own distinct flavor and aroma.


We take great care in packaging and shipping our teas to ensure their freshness and flavor, so that you can enjoy the true taste of Pu Erh tea in every cup. Join us on a journey through the rich and flavorful world of Pu Erh tea.
Shop our selection of organic, cooperative-grown Pu Erh teas today and taste the difference that quality and sustainability can make.
